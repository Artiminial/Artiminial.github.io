Site Purpose

The puropse of this site is to provide in-depth, accurate, up to date weather forecasts of a few cities within the United States. The main page will contain current weather stories about the cities that the site will cover. Copyright, current date, and terms of usage will be in the footer section When you click on the available towns, it will display the current town weather as well as any relevent articles or content related to the town including images that will reflect the current weather. The site will be responsive to screen size.
