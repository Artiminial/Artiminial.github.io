Target Audience

Our targeted audience are going to be the people who either live in or near these places, are visiting, or just driving through.

Persona 1

The first major group to use this website would be the people who live in these towns. They would need fast, up-to-date information about the area that they live in and the immediate surrounding area. The would also need updates and warnings about any severe local weather. These users are likley to use this site to plan large events, look for dangerous weather, and to plan out their day. The people who would use this site are local citizens as well as local govenment.

Persona 2

The second major group to use this website would be the people who will be traveling to or through the town. They will use the site to plan activities durring their visit as well as routes either through, or around the area based on the severity of the weather. These users will have similar expectations to the first person. They will expect fast, reliable weather forecasts as well as timley weather alerts and warnings. These people will usually be tourists, family of the locals, or meerly people who are passing through the town.

Scenarios

Scenario one: I want to plan a picnic for this weekend. What will the weather be like?

Scenario two: It is almost time to set up for the county fair, what week will the weather be best?

Scenario three: Severe weather is on its way. Am I at risk of flooding?

Scenario four: I am carrying a shipment of cargo to a neighboring town. Is the weather safe enough to pass through?

Scenario five: We are going to visit with our cousins. Will the weather be good enough to play soccer with them?
